    22→    22→### Backend API routes (all under src/app/api/)
    23→    23→| Method | Path | Purpose |
    24→    24→|---|---|---|
    25→    25→| POST | /api/seed | Seed demo data (idempotent). Body `{force?:boolean}` |
    26→    26→| GET | /api/plans | List plans (with `features` array) |
    27→    27→| GET | /api/marketplace?category=&q= | Public businesses list |
    28→    28→| GET/POST | /api/tenants | List (admin) / Register new business (auto-creates tenant+agent+knowledge+subscription+referral). POST body: `{businessType,name,description?,website?,instagram?,whatsapp?,phone?,address?,planCode?,ownerEmail,ownerName?}` |
    29→    29→| GET/PATCH | /api/tenants/[id] | Get/update tenant (PATCH fields: name,description,phone,address,instagram,website,accentColor,logoUrl,status,category) |
    30→    30→| POST | /api/auth/login | Body `{email,password}` → Session |
    31→    31→| GET/POST | /api/knowledge?tenantId= | List/create knowledge item. POST body: `{tenantId,type,title,content,question?,url?}`. type: faq\|pdf\|docx\|excel\|csv\|website\|text |
    32→    32→| DELETE | /api/knowledge/[id] | Delete knowledge item |
    33→    33→| POST | /api/chat | Body `{tenantId,conversationId?,message,history:ChatMessage[]}` → ChatResponse |
    34→    34→| GET/POST | /api/conversations?tenantId= | List conversations / operator reply (POST: `{conversationId,content}`) |
    35→    35→| GET | /api/conversations/[id]/messages | Messages of a conversation |
    36→    36→| GET/POST | /api/leads?tenantId= | List/create leads. POST: `{tenantId,conversationId?,name,phone,email?,source?,intent?}` |
    37→    37→| PATCH | /api/leads/[id] | Update lead (status,intent,name,phone,email,value) |
    38→    38→| GET | /api/analytics/[tenantId] | Full analytics bundle (kpis, usage, trends, channels, leadsByStatus, analysis) |
    39→    39→| GET/PATCH | /api/agent/[tenantId] | Get/update agent config. PATCH fields: name,systemPrompt,model,temperature,confidenceThreshold,greetingMessage,channels[],voiceEnabled,humanHandoff,growthLoop |
    40→    40→| GET | /api/admin/stats | Super-admin platform stats |
    41→    41→| GET/POST | /api/referral/[tenantId] | Get referral / record event `{event:"click"|"signup"}` |
    42→    42→| GET/POST | /api/bookings?tenantId= | List/create bookings |
